Shipping and Fulfillment for Your eBay Business

Learn about the different shipping options available for your eBay store, as well as tips for packaging and shipping items efficiently.

Shipping Options

There are several different shipping options available for your eBay store, including:

  • USPS First Class Mail
  • USPS Priority Mail
  • UPS Ground
  • FedEx Ground

Each shipping option has its own advantages and disadvantages, so it's important to choose the one that works best for your business and your customers.

Packaging Tips

Proper packaging is essential for ensuring that your items arrive at their destination safely and without damage. Some packaging tips to keep in mind include:

  • Use sturdy boxes or envelopes
  • Wrap fragile items in bubble wrap or packing peanuts
  • Use proper labeling and packing slips

By taking the time to package your items carefully, you can avoid costly returns and negative feedback from customers.
