eBay Reselling Excel Files

Excel files are an essential tool for eBay reselling. Here are some of the most useful Excel files you'll need to make your eBay reselling business a success:

Inventory Spreadsheet

An inventory spreadsheet is essential for keeping track of your eBay listings. You can use this spreadsheet to keep track of your item name, description, category, quantity, cost, selling price, and profit.

Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) Spreadsheet

A COGS spreadsheet is essential for tracking the cost of the goods you're selling. This spreadsheet will help you calculate your gross profit by subtracting the COGS from your total revenue.

Sales Report Spreadsheet

A sales report spreadsheet is essential for keeping track of your eBay sales. You can use this spreadsheet to keep track of your item name, selling price, quantity sold, and total revenue.

Expense Tracker Spreadsheet

An expense tracker spreadsheet is essential for tracking your eBay-related expenses. You can use this spreadsheet to keep track of your eBay fees, shipping costs, supplies, and other expenses.
