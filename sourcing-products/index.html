Sourcing Goods for Your eBay Business

Find out where to source products for your eBay store and learn how to evaluate potential products for profitability.

Local Thrift Stores

Thrift stores can be a great place to find unique and interesting items to resell on eBay. Some thrift stores even have special discount days where you can get even better deals.

Read More

Amazon Liquidation Centers

Amazon liquidation centers sell merchandise that Amazon has returned or can't sell. These items are often sold at a discount and can be a great source of inventory for your eBay store.

Read More

Bin Stores

Bin stores are discount stores that sell items by the pound. These stores can be a great source of inventory for your eBay store, as you can often find items that are in good condition but are no longer sold in regular retail stores.

Read More

Yard Sales

Yard sales can be a great place to find items to resell on eBay. People often sell items that they no longer need or want, and you can often negotiate a lower price than you would find at a regular retail store.
